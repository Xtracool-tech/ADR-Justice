ADR Justice Centre offers mediation services designed to facilitate constructive dialogue and negotiation between conflicting parties. We specialise in mediating in Family, Commercial, Housing/Disrepair, Faith, Education, Insurance, Civil, Workplace and other matters. Our experienced mediators guide participants through the mediation process, encouraging open communication and collaboration to reach mutually acceptable resolutions.

  1. Family Mediation: Facilitating constructive dialogue and negotiation to resolve family disputes, including issues related to divorce, child custody, and inheritance.
  2. Commercial Mediation: Assisting businesses in resolving commercial disputes such as contract breaches, partnership disagreements, and financial conflicts.
  3. Civil Mediation: Mediating disputes between individuals or entities outside of the commercial sphere, including property disputes, neighbor disputes, and personal injury claims.
