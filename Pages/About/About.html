Know more about ADR Justice Centre

About Us

The Arbitration and Mediation Justice Centre Ltd, commonly referred to as ADR Justice Centre, stands as a distinguished provider of alternative dispute resolution (ADR) services. Our organisation specialises in offering efficient and effective solutions for resolving conflicts outside the conventional realm of court proceedings. Guided by principles of impartiality, fairness, and professionalism, the ADR Justice Centre plays a pivotal role in facilitating arbitration and mediation processes, aiding individuals and organisations in reaching mutually beneficial resolutions across a diverse spectrum of disputes.

Our Vision

Our vision at ADR Justice Centre is to revolutionize dispute resolution, prioritizing collaboration, integrity, and efficiency. We strive to be the top choice for resolving conflicts with fairness, professionalism, and innovation, creating a world where disputes are settled amicably and transparently.

Core Values

At ADR Justice Centre, integrity, excellence, impartiality, and a client-centric approach form the foundation of our ethos. We uphold the highest standards of ethical conduct, delivering superior dispute resolution services through expertise, innovation, and adherence to international best practices. With a commitment to impartiality and neutrality, we prioritize our clients' needs, concerns, and objectives at every stage of the resolution process.

Our Mission

The mission of ADR Justice Centre is to promote the use of alternative dispute resolution mechanisms as efficient, cost-effective, and equitable alternatives to traditional litigation. By fostering a culture of collaboration, respect, and fairness, the Centre aims to empower individuals and organisations to resolve disputes amicably and preserve valuable relationships.

Comprehensive ADR Solutions We Render

With over a decade of expertise in successfully arbitrating, mediating and conciliating disputes, and professional advisory, the ADR Justice Centre boasts a comprehensive suite of ADR services. Our seasoned team, comprising experienced arbitrators, mediators, case managers, legal assistants, and mediation trainees, collaborates to guide parties toward effective and amicable solutions tailored to their unique needs. These services extend to personal, familial, commercial, civil, workplace, housing, and various other business interests, ensuring comprehensive coverage across multiple sectors. Central to our mission is the provision of complementary, voluntary, confidential, and accessible dispute resolution avenues. By empowering our service users with a range of options, we enable them to select the most fitting path for addressing any form of dispute that may arise. At the ADR Justice Centre, our commitment lies in fostering constructive dialogue, facilitating resolution, and fostering a culture of collaboration and understanding in conflict resolution endeavours.
